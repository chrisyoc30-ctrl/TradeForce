## Hashtag strategy

| Tag | Use when |
|-----|----------|
| **#TradeScore** | Always — brand recall. |
| **#Trades** | Tradesman-focused posts; join wider trade conversations. |
| **#HomeImprovement** | Homeowner posts; renovation / DIY adjacency. |
| **#SmallBusiness** | Pricing, fairness, “no subscription” angle; LinkedIn especially. |

**Optional (use sparingly, don’t stack more than ~4–5 per post):** `#Plumbers` `#Electricians` `#Glasgow` `#UKTrades` `#Renovation` — swap for your launch city or vertical.

**Avoid:** Irrelevant trending tags, hashtag walls, or banned/spammy patterns.

## Link strategy

- **Primary URL:** https://tradescore.uk (homepage or campaign landing).
- **UTM (recommended):** add parameters per channel so analytics stay clean, e.g.  
  `https://tradescore.uk/?utm_source=twitter&utm_medium=social&utm_campaign=launch`  
  `https://tradescore.uk/?utm_source=linkedin&utm_medium=social&utm_campaign=launch`  
  `https://tradescore.uk/?utm_source=facebook&utm_medium=social&utm_campaign=launch`
- **One clear CTA per post** — don’t split between three links.
- **Bio / Page:** keep the bare domain easy to type; use full UTM links in posts only.
